Introduction

I am a jewelry design expert, known for my creative designs and delicate drawings that encapsulate emotion. I deepened my understanding of various communication methods and design through my major in Mass Communication at Philippine Women's University. The core of my work lies in the details, and I strive to imbue each piece with my own stories and heart.

Experience

Jewelry Personal Brand (6 years)

Over the span of 6 years, I honed my skills in jewelry design, focusing intensely on developing a unique design philosophy and style. From conceptualization to production, I took charge of the entire process, ensuring a seamless journey from idea to finished product. A key aspect of my role was maintaining clear and effective communication with customers, understanding their preferences and needs to deliver bespoke pieces that resonated with them personally.

Fashion Jewelry Brand (2 years)

During my tenure at a fashion jewelry brand for 2 years, I had the opportunity to explore a wide range of materials and styles. This experience allowed me to innovate with designs that catered to accessible price points while staying abreast of current fashion trends. I thrived on the creative challenge of translating trends into wearable art, crafting pieces that were not only fashionable but also aligned with the brand's ethos of affordability and style.

Currently Employed at a Fine Jewelry Brand

I have recently transitioned back to the realm of fine jewelry, where I am currently employed. Here, my focus lies in the meticulous craftsmanship and luxurious design that define fine jewelry. My passion for this craft drives me to create pieces that go beyond mere adornment, aiming to capture and immortalize significant moments through intricate detailing and timeless elegance. This role allows me to delve deeply into the artistry of jewelry making, blending tradition with innovation to produce pieces of enduring beauty and significance.

In summary, my career journey in the jewelry industry has been marked by continuous growth and evolution, from establishing a personal brand rooted in unique design to exploring the dynamic landscape of fashion jewelry and now returning to the artistry of fine jewelry, where I am committed to creating pieces that resonate on a profound level with discerning clients.
